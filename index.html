About DISENFUTURED

THE BAND

DISENFUTURED is a New Jersey based punk band. We're probably a hardcore band, but we'll let the gatekeepers make their decision on that. Either way we draw on a lot of different influences and styles to make some music that is authentic to ourselves and is nominatively deterministic, i.e., the music itself should make you feel disenfutured. Whatever the fuck that means.

MEMBERS:

  • Guitar/Vocals - Ben Mazza
  • Keys/Vocals - Jack Andrus
  • Guitar - Sam Beson
  • Bass - Noah Lipton
  • Drums - Nico Agostini
